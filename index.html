Why Camping?

Want more fun?? Then go for camping!!! Camping is one of the most elegant ways to enjoy nature with our family and friends. It gives a lot of fun and helps to explore the places to the fullest. The warm bonfires, the peaceful silence and the bright mornings make camping a must-have experience for different kinds of travelers. One of the best places for camping that can be visited by both friends and family is Mussoorie. Mussoorie, otherwise called ‘Queen of Hills’ is at an altitude of 7000 feet above sea level. So the climate is always pleasant and cold throughout the year, which makes camping in the hills all the more fun.
